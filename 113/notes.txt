Minimum Wage by US State (1968-2018)

This video shows the changes in basic minimum wages in non-farm employment under state law for each state in the United States from 1968 to 2018. The dollar values are as they were in their respective year and have not been adjusted for inflation.

Note: Some of the data had the minimum wage set to be within a range. In this case, the higher of the two range values was taken. There were ranges due to different restrictions varying by state and can be read in detail here: https://www.dol.gov/whd/state/stateMinWageHis.htm
