Coronavirus: Total tests per 1000 people performed over the last 2 months by country [1st March to 30th April]

[Updated as of 5th May 2020 at 7.45 ET]

This video ranks countries based on the number of tests performed for coronavirus(COVID-19) per 1000 population since March 1, 2020 up until April 30, 2020. While there isn't any way to realize the actual number of infected people from the test data, it helps us understand the quality of a country's data and reporting methods on the pandemic.
