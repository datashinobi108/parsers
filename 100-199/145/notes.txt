Ranking Countries according to Deaths per Million People [11 April 2020]

[Updated as of 11th April 2020 at 7.45 ET]

This video ranks countries based on the average number of cases/people confirmed to have died from the coronavirus(COVID-19) per 1 million people since Mar 1, 2020 up until April 11, 2020. The virus has spread to over 180 countries. I also added in the world average in the animation for comparison.
The number of reported deaths in Europe keeps on rising especially in Italy, Spain, France and UK, four countries that have overtaken China.

[Source]
World Health Organization (WHO), CDC
