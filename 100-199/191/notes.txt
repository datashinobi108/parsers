Top countries by consumption-based CO₂ emissions [1990 to 2017]

Figures shown are measured in million tonnes

This video shows the top countries according to consumption-based CO₂ emissions from 1990 to 2017. Consumption-based emissions are adjusted for trade. What this means is emissions caused abroad by imported goods are included where as emissions caused by exports to another country are excluded.
