Ranking Countries according to Reported Deaths [9 April 2020]

[Updated as of 9th April 2020 at 7.15 ET]

This video shows the top 20 countries according to the number of people confirmed to have died from the coronavirus(COVID-19) since March 1, 2020 up until April 9, 2020.
The number of reported deaths is lower than the number of actual total deaths. The main reason for this is due to limited testing and reporting methodologies(or lack thereof) that varies from country to country.
The virus has spread to over 180 countries with significant outbreaks in China (where the virus originated), Italy, Spain, Germany, France and USA.

[Source]
World Health Organization (WHO), CDC
