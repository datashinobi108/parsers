Ranking Countries according to Daily Reported Cases

[Updated as of 4th April 2020 at 11.30 ET]

This video ranks countries based on the number of daily confirmed cases due to the coronavirus(COVID-19) since March 1, 2020 up until April 4, 2020. The virus has spread to 180 countries with significant outbreaks in China (where the virus originated), Italy, Spain and USA.
The number of confirmed cases is lower than the number of actual total cases. The main reason for this is due to limited testing that varies from country to country.

[Source]
World Health Organization (WHO), CDC, Johns Hopkins University
