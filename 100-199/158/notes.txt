Coronavirus: Total tests performed over the last 2 months by country [1st March to 30th April]

[Updated as of 3rd May 2020 at 7.45 ET]

This video ranks countries based on the total number of people that have been tested for coronavirus(COVID-19) since March 1, 2020 up until April 30, 2020. While there isn't any way to realize the actual number of infected people from the test data, it helps us understand the quality of a country's data and reporting methods on the pandemic.

[Source]
World Health Organization (WHO), CDC, Johns Hopkins University

Comment:
It is worth noting that these numbers are not accurate and do not represent what is actually happening. This could be due to:
1) countries not being able to test as many people
2) countries not recording the number of tests accurately
