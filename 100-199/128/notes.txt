Most watched super bowls of all time

This video ranks all the super bowls according to average TV audience from Super Bowl 1 to Super Bowl 54. Viewership peaked in 2015 where the Patriots won the game against the Seattle Seahawks and it was watched by over 114 million people. The Pittsburgh Steelers and the New England Patriots have won the most Super Bowl titles with a total of six each.
