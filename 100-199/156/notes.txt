Coronavirus: Ranking Countries according to Cases per Million People [30 April 2020]

[Updated as of 30th April 2020 at 7.45 ET]

This video ranks countries based on the average number of cases/people confirmed to have the coronavirus(COVID-19) per 1 million people since Mar 1, 2020 up until April 30, 2020. The virus has spread to over 180 countries. I also added in the world average in the animation for comparison.
The number of confirmed cases keeps on rising especially in USA, Italy, Spain, France, UK, Turkey and Germany.

[Source]
World Health Organization (WHO), CDC, Johns Hopkins University
