Coronavirus: Ranking of Daily Reported Deaths by Country [23 April 2020]

[Updated as of 23rd April 2020 at 8.45 ET]

This video ranks countries based on the number of daily confirmed cases due to the coronavirus(COVID-19) since March 1, 2020 up until April 23, 2020.
The virus has spread to over 180 countries with significant outbreaks in China (where the virus originated), Italy, Spain, Germany, France and USA.
The number of confirmed cases is lower than the number of actual total cases. The main reason for this is due to limited testing that varies from country to country.

[Source]
World Health Organization (WHO), CDC, Johns Hopkins University
