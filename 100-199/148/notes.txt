Ranking Countries according to Total Tests Performed per 1000 people [15 April 2020]

[Updated as of 15th April 2020 at 7.45 ET]

This video ranks countries based on the number of tests performed for coronavirus(COVID-19) per 1000 population since Feb 1, 2020 up until April 15, 2020. While there isn't any way to realize the actual number of infected people from the test data, it helps us understand the quality of a country's data and reporting methods on the pandemic.
