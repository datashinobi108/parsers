Ranking Countries according to Reported Deaths

[Updated as of 26th March 2020 at 7.30 ET]

This video shows the top 25 countries according to the number of deaths that have been reported due to coronavirus(COVID-19) since February 1, 2020 up until March 26, 2020.
The number of reported deaths in Europe keeps on rising especially in Italy and Spain, two countries that have overtaken China. The virus has now spread to over 170 countries with significant outbreaks in China (where the virus originated), Italy, Spain, US, Iran and South Korea.

[Source]
World Health Organization (WHO), CDC
