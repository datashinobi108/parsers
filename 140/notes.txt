Ranking Countries according to Daily Reported Deaths

[Updated as of 2nd April 2020 at 11.45 ET]

This video ranks countries based on the number of cases/people confirmed to have the coronavirus(COVID-19) per 1 million people since March 1, 2020 up until April 2, 2020. The virus has spread to 180 countries with significant outbreaks in China (where the virus originated), Italy, Spain and USA.
The number of reported deaths in Europe keeps on rising especially in Italy and Spain, two countries that have overtaken China.

[Source]
World Health Organization (WHO), CDC, Johns Hopkins University
