Ranking Countries according to Daily Reported Deaths

[Updated as of 2nd April 2020 at 11.45 ET]

This video ranks countries based on the number of daily reported deaths due to the coronavirus(COVID-19) since March 1, 2020 up until April 2, 2020. The virus has spread to 180 countries with significant outbreaks in China (where the virus originated), Italy, Spain and USA.
The number of reported deaths around the world is increasing at an alarming rate. Just over March alone, the world went from ~70 deaths per day to ~5000 deaths per day.

[Source]
World Health Organization (WHO), CDC, Johns Hopkins University
