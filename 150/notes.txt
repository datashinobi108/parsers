Coronavirus: Ranking Countries according to Reported Deaths [19 April 2020]

[Updated as of 19 April 2020 at 11.45 ET]

This video shows the top 15 countries according to the number of people confirmed to have died from the coronavirus(COVID-19) since March 1, 2020 up until April 19, 2020.
The number of reported deaths is lower than the number of actual total deaths. The main reason for this is due to limited testing and reporting methodologies(or lack thereof) that varies from country to country.
The virus has spread to 180 countries with significant outbreaks in China (where the virus originated), Italy, Spain and USA.

[Source]
World Health Organization (WHO), CDC, Johns Hopkins University
