COVID-19 Average Cases by Country compared to World Average | Coronavirus

[Updated as of 17th March 2020]

This video ranks countries based on the average number of cases/people confirmed to have the coronavirus(COVID-19) per 1 million people since January 21, 2020 up until March 13, 2020. The virus has spread to over 140 countries with significant outbreaks in China (where the virus originated), Italy, Iran and South Korea. I also added in the world average in the animation for comparison.
