Coronavirus: 4.5 million cases by country [16th May 2020]

[Updated as of 16th May 2020 at 7.45 ET]

This video shows the top countries according to the number of cases/people confirmed to have the coronavirus(COVID-19) since January 29, 2020 up until May 16, 2020.
The number of confirmed cases is lower than the number of actual total cases. The main reason for this is due to limited testing that varies from country to country.
The virus has spread to over 180 countries with significant outbreaks in China (where the virus originated), Russia, UK, Canada, Italy, Spain, Germany, France and USA.

[Source]
World Health Organization (WHO), CDC, Johns Hopkins University
