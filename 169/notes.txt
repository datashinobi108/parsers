Top countries by coffee bean production [1961 to 2018]

Figures shown are measured in tonnes

This video show the top countries according to the amount of coffee that is produced each year. Data are expressed in terms of area harvested, production quantity and yield which mainly targets crops grown in field and market gardens mainly for sale, therefore excluding crops grown in kitchen or small family gardens that are for household consumption.

[Source]
Food and Agriculture Organization of the United Nations (FAO) (2020)
