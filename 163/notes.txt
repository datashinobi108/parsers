Coronavirus: 320k deaths by country [20th May 2020]

[Updated as of 20th May 2020 at 7.45 ET]

This video shows the top countries according to the number of people confirmed to have died from the coronavirus(COVID-19) since January 29, 2020 up until May 20, 2020.
The number of reported deaths is lower than the number of actual total deaths. The main reason for this is due to limited testing and reporting methodologies(or lack thereof) that varies from country to country.
The virus has spread to over 180 countries with significant outbreaks in China (where the virus originated), Russia, UK, Canada, Italy, Spain, Germany, France and USA.

[Source]
World Health Organization (WHO), CDC, Johns Hopkins University
