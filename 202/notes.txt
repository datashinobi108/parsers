Coronavirus update: Daily new deaths by country [16th October 2020]

[Updated as of 16th October 2020 at 7.45 ET]

This video ranks countries based on the number of daily reported deaths due to the coronavirus(COVID-19) since January 21, 2020 up until October 16, 2020. The virus has spread to 180 countries with significant outbreaks in China (where the virus originated), Italy, Spain and USA.
The number of confirmed deaths is lower than the number of actual total deaths. The main reason for this is due to limited testing that varies from country to country.
The number of reported deaths around the world is increasing at an alarming rate. Just over March alone, the world went from ~70 deaths per day to ~5000 deaths per day.

[Source]
World Health Organization (WHO), CDC, Johns Hopkins University
