Ranking Countries according to Cases per Million People [12 April 2020]

[Updated as of 12th April 2020 at 7.45 ET]

This video ranks countries based on the average number of cases/people confirmed to have the coronavirus(COVID-19) per 1 million people since Mar 1, 2020 up until April 12, 2020. The virus has spread to over 180 countries. I also added in the world average in the animation for comparison.
The number of confirmed cases keeps on rising especially in USA, Italy, Spain, France and Germany, four countries that have overtaken China.

[Source]
World Health Organization (WHO), CDC
