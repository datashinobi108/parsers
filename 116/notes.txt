Countries with the Most Fatalities from Natural Disasters (1990-2017)

This video shows the total number of fatalities(deaths) caused by natural disasters. Natural disasters include earthquakes, cyclones, floods, extreme weather, extreme temparature, wildfires, volcanic activity, drought, landslides.

Some of the biggest natural disaster events include:
- The 1991 Bangladesh Cyclone
- The 2004 Indian Ocean Earthquake and Tsunami
- The 2008 Sichuan Earthquake
- The 2010 Haiti Earthquake

Institute for Health Metrics and Evaluation (IHME)
