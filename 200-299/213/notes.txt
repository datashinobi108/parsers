Top Global Brands [2000 - 2020]

This video shows the top global brands from 2000 to 2020 according to an annual report of the worlds most valuable brands published by Interbrand, a New York based marketing consultancy. To qualify, a company/brand has to have a presence on at least three major continents and must have broad geographic coverage in growing and emerging markets.
