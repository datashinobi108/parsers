COVID-19 in Europe: Over 18 million confirmed cases [4 December 2020]

[Updated as of 4th December 2020 at 7.45 ET]

This video shows the top countries in Europe according to the number of people confirmed to have the coronavirus(COVID-19) since February 1, 2020 up until December 4, 2020.
The number of confirmed cases is lower than the number of actual total cases. The main reason for this is due to limited testing that varies from country to country.

[Source]
World Health Organization (WHO), CDC, Johns Hopkins University
