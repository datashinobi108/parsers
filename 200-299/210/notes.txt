COVID-19 in The Americas: Over 30 million confirmed cases [11 December 2020]

[Updated as of 11th December 2020 at 7.45 ET]

This video shows the top countries in the Americas(North America, South America and the Carribean) according to the number of people confirmed to have the coronavirus(COVID-19) since February 1, 2020 up until December 11, 2020.
The number of confirmed cases is lower than the number of actual total cases. The main reason for this is due to limited testing that varies from country to country.

[Source]
World Health Organization (WHO), CDC, Johns Hopkins University
