Richest People in the World 2021

In this video, we look at the world's richest people since 2010. Given that Elon Musk has now become the world's richest person, I thought it would be cool to see how he shot up to the top in such a short time. This list is based on the documented net worth of the wealthiest billionaires in the world, published every year in March by Forbes. I have added in the real-time list for 2021 to account for the latest standings which put Elon Musk as the richest person in the world right now.
