Ranking Countries according to Total Tests Performed [13 April 2020]

[Updated as of 13th April 2020 at 7.45 ET]

This video ranks countries based on the total number of people that have been tested for coronavirus(COVID-19) since Feb 1, 2020 up until April 13, 2020. While there isn't any way to realize the actual number of infected people from the test data, it helps us understand the quality of a country's data and reporting methods on the pandemic.

[Source]
World Health Organization (WHO), CDC

Comment:
It is worth noting that these numbers are not accurate and do not represent what is actually happening. This could be due to:
1) countries not being able to test as many people
2) countries not recording the number of tests correctly
